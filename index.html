About Us

CozyCup Café was born from the passion of two IT students, Jasper Saez and Zuriel Montallana, who wanted to create a welcoming space for great coffee and relaxation. We're committed to offering quality, locally sourced coffee and freshly baked pastries, all in a cozy, community-focused atmosphere. At CozyCup Café, we believe in serving more than just coffee, we're serving moments of connection and comfort.
